Football

    About Football :

    Football is a team sport played between two teams of eleven players each playing a ball .The goalkeeper is the only player permitted to touch the ball with his hands or arms, provided he is inside the penalty area. Non-goalkeepers often use their legs to attack or pass the ball and can also use their head to hit the ball. The style of playing football developed throughout history, as well as its regulations and laws, until it reached the current stage known as modern football, The FIFA World Cup is the largest international forum in the field of this sport, and every country in the world aspires to win it

Volleyball

    About Volleyball :

    On February 9, 1895 AD, in Holyoke, Massachusetts, William George Morgan, Director of Physical Education at the YMCA, created a new sports game called Mentonite to pass the time in an entertaining manner, and he preferred to play this game indoors and with any number of players. The game took some of its characteristics from badminton, tennis and handball, at a time when basketball was a new sport. Volleyball was created with the understanding that it is the less rough indoor sport than YMCA member-friendly basketball.

    About Stadiam :

    The playing area consists of the playing field and the free zone, and the playing area must be rectangular and symmetrical. The playground is a rectangular shape, its length is eighteen meters, and its width is nine meters. The center line is drawn at a distance of three meters from the center line on each side a line parallel to the center line, dividing each square into two areas, the area near the network is called the offensive zone, and the second zone is the defense zone. The transmission area is determined by a line of 15 cm long and 20 cm away from the finish line. It is preferable to play volleyball in a closed hall with a ceiling height of no less than seven meters.
